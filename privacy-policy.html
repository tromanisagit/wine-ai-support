Privacy First: Wine AI is designed with privacy at its core. All AI processing happens on your device - your conversations and data never leave your phone.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

The App does not collect or store personal information you input, such as:

  • Conversations with the AI sommelier
  • Wine quiz answers or scores
  • Cellar information and wine ratings
  • Photos of wine labels or menus

All data is processed and stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

To operate the App and comply with App Store requirements, we may automatically collect limited information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics on app performance and crash reports
  • Subscription Data: Purchase status processed through Apple's App Store

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Ensure compatibility with your device
  • Monitor and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Manage subscriptions and provide access to premium features

3. Data Security & Privacy

3.1 On-Device Processing

The App's AI sommelier operates entirely on-device using a local language model. Your interactions with the AI are never transmitted to external servers.

3.2 Web Search Features

When web search is enabled, Wine AI may send your search queries to Google Custom Search API. You must provide your own Google API credentials, which are stored securely in your device's keychain. You can disable web search at any time.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

Subscription data is managed by Apple's App Store - we do not have access to your payment details.

5. Your Control

You can manage your data through the App's settings:

  • Delete locally stored chat history or cellar information
  • Control device permissions through iOS settings
  • Adjust subscription preferences via the App Store

6. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at: wineaisommelier@icloud.com
